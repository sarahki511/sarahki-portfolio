About me

Hello! My name is Sarah and I am an aspiring software engineer. I graduated from UCSD with a Bioengineering: Bioinformatics degree. My interest in software engineering began when I entered college. I did not know anything about coding until I took my first in Introductory course to Java my freshman year. It was quite shocking to see that I had to take ANY coding classes. But soon, I found out that most of my classes will be focused in computer science engineering classes. I was quite distressed since I did not have any exposure to coding.
After investing a years learning different coding languages (e.g. Java, C++, Python, etc) and algorithms, I became interested in becoming a software engineer. I’ve been constantly taking online course to help me improve my technical abilities related to creating web applications.
